Farm Labour

Finding reliable, skilled farm workers in Nigeria can be one of the biggest challenges for farmers and agribusinesses. Premaxx Agro Tech maintains a network of vetted, trained agricultural workers ready to be deployed to farms across Rivers State and beyond.

From seasonal field hands to experienced farm supervisors and specialist agronomists, we match the right people to the right farms — saving you time, reducing recruitment headaches and ensuring productivity on your land.

  • Seasonal farm workers and field hands
  • Farm supervisors and team leaders
  • Agronomists and crop specialists
  • Machinery operators
  • All workers vetted and trained
  • Short-term and long-term placements
  • Available for farms of all sizes

Farmland Lease

Premaxx Agro Tech offers fertile agricultural land for lease across Rivers State — giving farmers and agribusinesses the opportunity to scale their operations without the high cost of land acquisition.

  • Various plot sizes available
  • Locations across Rivers State
  • Flexible short and long-term lease terms
  • Suitable for arable and cash crops
  • Clear title documentation provided
  • Available to individuals and corporates
