Our Story

Who We Are

Premaxx Agro Tech LTD is a Port Harcourt based agricultural services company committed to modernising farming in Rivers State and the wider Niger Delta. We were founded with a simple but powerful belief: that Nigerian farmers deserve access to the same quality inputs, machinery and expertise as any world-class farm operation.

Founded in 2021, we have grown into a trusted agro-tech partner for smallholder farmers, commercial agribusinesses, and agricultural entrepreneurs across Rivers State. Our team brings together expertise in agronomy, agricultural engineering, logistics and business development — all focused on one goal: helping Nigerian farms produce more.

Based in Port Harcourt with deep roots in the communities we serve, Premaxx understands the unique challenges and opportunities of farming in the Niger Delta. From waterlogged terrains to seasonal crop cycles, we design our solutions with the Nigerian farmer in mind.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide Nigerian farmers and agribusinesses with reliable, affordable and modern agricultural services — from machinery hire and quality agro inputs to skilled farm labour and premium farmland — enabling them to farm smarter, produce more and earn better.

Our Vision

To become West Africa's most trusted one-stop agro-tech service provider — transforming rural agriculture through innovation, accessibility and a deep commitment to the communities that feed our nation.
